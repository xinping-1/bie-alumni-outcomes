Purpose of this Section

This section is designed to help you explore what’s possible with a Bachelor of International Economics. By browsing real career outcomes from BIE alum (Classes of 2025–2019), we hope to help you dream big about where your degree can take you — both during your studies and after graduation.

How to Use This Resource

Review the jobs and organizations below with curiosity. Look for patterns, industries, and roles that interest you. Use this page as a starting point for exploration, not a checklist.

  • Search by job title, organization, or industry
  • Filter by graduation year to see trends across cohorts
  • Use tags to identify skills, functions, and career areas
  • Look for roles that spark interest — then research them further!

Many positions listed here were held as co-op placements, internships, part-time work during school, and full-time roles after graduation.

What Categories Mean

Each role is assigned a primary category based on its main responsibilities. Additional tags provide more detail — including the skills involved, the work environment, and where it overlaps with other fields.

Data Disclaimer

This data is primarily self-reported and includes information publicly collected through LinkedIn and graduate surveys. It reflects the roles held by alum at the time of collection, and may not represent their current positions.
