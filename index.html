Purpose of this Section

This section highlights the career paths of BIE alumni from the Classes of 2025 to 2019. It helps current BIE students understand potential opportunities after graduation and sparks ideas for their own career journeys.

How to Use This Resource

Review the jobs and organizations below with curiosity. Look for patterns, industries, and roles that interest you. Use this page as a starting point for exploration, not a checklist.

What You Will Gain from This Section

  • Awareness of where BIE alumni from each graduating year have been hired
  • Insight into the types of roles available to BIE graduates
  • Ideas to guide your career discovery, networking, and internship search

How We Created Categories

Each job is assigned a primary category based on its main function. Additional tags are included to show overlap between fields.

Data Disclaimer

This data is self-reported and reflects the roles held by alumni at the time of collection. It provides examples of possible career paths but is not exhaustive of all alumni outcomes.
